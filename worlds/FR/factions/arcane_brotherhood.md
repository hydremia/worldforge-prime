## 1. Overview

**Full Name:** The Arcane Brotherhood  
**Common Names/Aliases:** The Brotherhood

**One-Sentence Summary:**
A cabal of ambitious mages centered in Luskan, seeking power, knowledge, and control over northern trade and arcane secrets.

**Longer Summary:**
The Arcane Brotherhood is a notorious order of wizards whose internal rivalries are matched only by their shared desire to dominate northern politics and magic. Though often at each other’s throats, they present a formidable front when pursuing external goals.

---
## 2. Origins & History (High-Level)

- Rose to influence in Luskan as a mage-governed power structure.
- Experienced cycles of coups, collapses, and resurgences.
- By the 1490s DR, they are again a major but unstable force in the North.

---
## 3. Ideology, Goals & Methods

### 3.1 Ideology
- Magical power is an end in itself and a means to influence.
- The strong and clever deserve to shape the world.

### 3.2 Goals
- Secure magical sites, artifacts, and secrets throughout the North.
- Leverage Luskan as a hub of arcane and mercantile power.

### 3.3 Methods
- Espionage, manipulation of local politics, arcane intimidation.
- Sponsoring expeditions into dangerous, magic-rich locations.

---
## 4. Structure & Organization

### 4.1 Leadership
- Led by powerful archmages, often in tense alliances.

### 4.2 Internal Structure
- Apprenticeships, rival cliques, and secret research circles.

### 4.3 Membership
- Wizards, some warlocks and other arcane casters.

---
## 5. Presence & Influence

### 5.1 Primary Regions of Operation
- Luskan, the Spine of the World, Icewind Dale.

### 5.2 Public Perception
- Feared, distrusted, occasionally admired for their arcane might.

### 5.3 Resources & Capabilities
- Towers, libraries, spell laboratories, bound creatures.

---
## 6. Relationships with Other Factions

- **Harpers:** Often targets of Harper investigation and opposition.
- **Zhentarim:** Pragmatic partners or rivals in northern schemes.

---
## 7. Symbols, Colors & Iconography

- **Heraldry / symbol:** Stylized tower, eye, or arcane sigil linked to Luskan.

---
## 8. Typical Agents & Tactics in Play

- Patron or antagonist to adventuring parties exploring ruins.

---
## 9. Adventure Hooks

- PCs hired to recover a lost spellbook or artifact.
- PCs caught in a power struggle between rival Brotherhood mages.

---
## 10. Canon Notes & Variants

- **Baseline Canon:** Self-interested, ambitious cabal.
- **Campaign Variants:** Ranging from outright villains to morally ambiguous patrons.
