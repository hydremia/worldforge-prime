## 1. Overview

**Full Name:** The Cult of the Dragon  
**Common Names/Aliases:** The Dragon Cult

**One-Sentence Summary:**
A fanatical cult venerating dragons and seeking to bring about dragon-dominated ages.

**Longer Summary:**
The Cult of the Dragon shifts between fragmented cells and large-scale conspiracies, always centered on empowering dragons—living or undead—to rule. Their methods range from quiet worship and artifact hunting to continent-shaking rituals.

---
## 2. Origins & History (High-Level)

- Originated from prophecies about undead dragons ruling the world.
- Has reinterpreted its goals multiple times (dracoliches vs. living dragon supremacy).

---
## 3. Ideology, Goals & Methods

### 3.1 Ideology
- Dragons are rightful apex rulers.
- Mortal lives are expendable tools for draconic ascendance.

### 3.2 Goals
- Gather wealth, magic, and artifacts to empower allied dragons.
- Resurrect, resurrect, or elevate dragon overlords.

### 3.3 Methods
- Cult cells embedded in cities, remote shrines, and lairs.
- Sacrifices, dark rituals, and infiltration.

---
## 4. Structure & Organization

### 4.1 Leadership
- Dragon-worshipping high priests, wyrmspeakers, and favored champions.

### 4.2 Internal Structure
- Cells devoted to specific dragons or draconic goals.

### 4.3 Membership
- Fanatics, opportunists, dragonborn, and others drawn to draconic power.

---
## 5. Presence & Influence

- Fractured but persistent presence across many regions.

---
## 6. Relationships with Other Factions

- **Harpers & Gauntlet:** Active enemies.
- **Zhentarim:** Occasional collaborators or tools.

---
## 7. Symbols, Colors & Iconography

- **Heraldry / symbol:** Dragon fangs, eyes, or skulls.

---
## 8. Adventure Hooks
