## 1. Overview

**Full Name:** The Red Wizards of Thay  
**Common Names/Aliases:** Red Wizards, Thayan Enclaves

**One-Sentence Summary:**
A cruel magocracy of necromancers and arcane tyrants from Thay, extending their reach through enclaves, trade, and fear.

**Longer Summary:**
The Red Wizards rule Thay through fear, undeath, and absolute magical control. Outside their homeland, they present a more polished, mercantile face, operating magical enclaves that sell arcane services while pursuing hidden agendas.

---
## 2. Ideology, Goals & Methods

- Power and control through magic, especially necromancy.
- Export fear and influence via "legitimate" enclaves.

---
## 3. Structure & Organization

- Ruled by the Zulkirs and their inner circles.
- Rigid hierarchy enforced by magical oaths and terror.

---
## 4. Presence & Influence

- Strongest in Thay; enclaves found across Faerûn.

---
## 5. Adventure Hooks

- PCs hired by or against a Thayan enclave with murky goals.

---
## 6. Canon Notes & Variants

- **Baseline Canon:** Classic Thayan tyranny with 5e-style enclaves.
