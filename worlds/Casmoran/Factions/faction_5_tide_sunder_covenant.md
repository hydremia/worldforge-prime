## **Summary**
The Tide-Sunder Covenant stands between Casmoran and another collapse of reality. They contain rifts, bleed planar pressure, and shut down arcane catastrophes before they escalate. They are feared, respected, and often misunderstood.

---

## **Origins & Shattering Legacy**
Formed in the immediate aftermath of the Shattering by survivors from Zephrix clerics, Basturion bulwark orders, and Mirethiel flow-keepers. Their founding creed: **"Better a controlled wound than a mortal one."**

---

## **Structure**
- **Riftwardens:** Crisis leaders.
- **Sunder-Engineers:** Designers of containment systems.
- **Tidecallers:** Ritual redirection specialists.
- **Bulwark Keepers:** Defensive stabilizers.
- **Gloamwatch Scouts:** Wild-magic trackers.

---

## **Methods**
- Controlled rift-bleeding.
- Reality-scaffolding constructs.
- Demolition of unstable structures.
- Arcane evacuation coordination.
- Hazard mapping across regions.
- Containment ward circles.

## **Goals**
- **Short-Term:** Halt an Inner Sea riftstorm; train Dawnwall scouts; seal Peninsulas fracture-nodes.
